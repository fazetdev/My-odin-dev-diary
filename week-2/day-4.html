🟣 Day 4 – Pause, Retreat, Recharge

Today I hit a wall. After days of non-stop coding, learning, and struggling with Flexbox, I realized my brain wasn’t absorbing anything anymore. I opened the Flexbox assignments again, stared at them for a while, and felt nothing but confusion.

That’s when I knew — I needed a break.

Instead of forcing my way through, I decided to pause and take the day off. Not just from the keyboard, but mentally too. No tutorials, no Git battles, no layout drama. Just rest.

It felt a bit guilty at first, but I reminded myself: burnout doesn't build progress. Reflection does. Stepping back today means coming back sharper tomorrow.

Sometimes the most productive thing you can do... is stop.

✅ Wins:

  • Recognized signs of burnout early
  • Chose rest over forcing unproductive work

⚠️ Problem:

Mental fatigue made it hard to retain or apply Flexbox concepts

💡 Realization:

Rest isn’t weakness — it’s a strategy for sustainability

Day 4: logged, rested, and ready to return stronger. 🌱
