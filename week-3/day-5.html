🔴 Day 5 – Building My Personal Project

After successfully submitting the Odin Landing Page project, I shifted my focus to something close to heart — Tsire Hub, my personal landing page. I decided today would be all about refining it, making it neat, and applying everything I’ve learned so far from HTML and CSS.

Unlike the Odin project, this felt more relaxed and creative. I wasn’t chasing pixel perfection or comparison screenshots. Instead, I focused on organizing my content, giving it a clean layout, and making sure the design matched the vibe of what I want Tsire Hub to represent.

The best part? I didn’t rush. I took time reviewing each section, fixing alignment, and reusing some styles and techniques I had practiced earlier. By the end of the day, the site felt more polished — like a proper digital handshake for anyone who stumbles upon it.

  • ✅ Success: Started and styled Tsire Hub with more intention and calm.
  • ⚠️ Challenge: Avoiding overthinking every small detail.
  • 💡 Solution: Reminded myself that personal projects evolve — it's better to build and improve than to freeze waiting for perfection.
